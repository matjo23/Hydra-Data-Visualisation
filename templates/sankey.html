Patient Pathways Visualisation

These sankey diagrams illustrate the complex relationships and pathways between different aspects of the historical medical data collected at Craiglockhart War Hospital during World War I.

Understanding the Visualisation

These Sankey diagrams maps the flow of patients through various aspects of their medical journey at Craiglockhart War Hospital. The width of each connection represents the volume of patients following that particular path.

By examining these flows, we can identify patterns in treatment duration, age groups, regiments, common diagnoses, and patient outcomes during this critical period of military care.
