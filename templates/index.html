Historical Context

Craiglockhart War Hospital was a British military psychiatric hospital for officers during World War I. Located in Edinburgh, Scotland, it treated soldiers suffering from shell shock/neurasthenia (now known as PTSD) and other psychological trauma from combat.

This visualization project provides insights into treatment methodologies, patient demographics, and recovery outcomes during this critical period in medical history.
